Course Outcome

After completion of the course, the trainee should be able to:

  • Understand network technologies and how devices access local and remote network resources.
  • Understand router hardware and how switching operates in a small to medium-sized business network.
  • Understand an IP addressing scheme to provide network connectivity for a small to medium-sized business network.
  • Understand initial settings on a network device and implement basic network connectivity between devices.
  • Understand monitoring tools and network management protocols to troubleshoot data networks.
  • Understand Ethernet switch ports concept and evaluate VLANs.
